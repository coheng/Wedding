Intermediate Software Engineer

Location: Sandton, Johannesburg

Job Purpose

• To implement, guided by the technical delivery roadmap • To manage the workload of Intermediate / Junior developers • To provide guidance on technical / support issues (escalation route) • To liaise with users regarding feature requests • To ensure compliance with coding guidelines • To ensure quality of product delivery

Responsibilities, Activities and Key Deliverables

Product Environment • Features released on schedule • Assist in roadmap creation Support Environment • Escalation route when Support Developers are stuck • Assist in supporting product partners Testing Environment • Unit tests up to date • Ensure turnaround (via testers) Team Quality Assurance • All code produced within team is aligned with defined quality and technical standards Development & Build Environments • In-depth understanding of C# development and build environments • In-depth understanding of the applied branching strategy and adhering to it • Assist team in setup and troubleshooting • Suggest latest / improved ways of implementation • Good understanding of the client platforms to be supported Architectural Concepts • In-depth understanding of Object Orientation • Set standard of concept usage for team Development Languages • Expert knowledge of relevant technology stack e.g. C#, Angular etc • Expert knowledge of the .NET Framework incl. versioning • Good knowledge of SQL and its Management Studio • Familiar with supporting tools e.g. Resharper and their value add Test Driven Development • Articulate value add to get buy in from team and management • Produce timelines cognisant of extra time required Agile Concepts & Relevant Artifacts • Familiar with latest concepts • Application of process and artefacts where beneficial to product

Job Specific Qualifications and Experience

Qualifications 1. IT related degree / diploma required 2. Project Management beneficial Experience 3. At least 8 years in technical IT environment 4. Experience in doing software development 5. Experience in software product development

Behavioral Competencies

Communication • Team up to date with what is required • Management up to date with progress • Clients informed on new releases and support tickets Time Management • Availability to team • Timeous delivery of artefacts responsible for • Guides / mentors team in management of their time Organisational • Team familiar and apply process • Monitoring of activity, adjust where necessary

Values & Attributes

Honesty • Clear message on change and reasons for it • Communicate accurate progress updates • Direct performance guidance / reviews Positive Attitude • Display enthusiasm at all times • Nurture team morale • Escalate potential negativity where necessary Team Player • Include the team with decisions where possible • Always keep the team in the loop of information • Be cognisant of effect on team • Support where possible and facilitate where necessary Empathy • Pay respect to human nature • Care about well-being of team • Escalate potential issues where necessary • Display kindness Organic • Change as necessary • Evolve • Manage team on individual needs
